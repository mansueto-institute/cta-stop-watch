StopWatch: Exploring Bus Service in Chicago

The Mansueto StopWatch — part of 2024 Mansueto Data Journalism Initiative— investigate Chciago Transit Authority (CTA) bus service after the COVID-19 pandemic disruptions. The StopWatch analyzes historic real time bus location data as collected by the Chi Hack Night Ghost Bus since May 2022 to date. The goal was to understand how bus service changed in the city of Chicago over time and across the city’s community areas.

As part of the project we have provide the Bus Report Cards — an interactive platform with indicators at the community, route and bus stop level to allow riders to explore relevant metrics about the stops and routes that they use. The report cards will update monthly with up to date metrics.

Reviewing the real time bus locations as compared to the schedule, we found that the CTA initially opted to decrease scheduled service to match performance and then increased it, but these changes were disproportionately felt across Chicago’s community areas.
